NATURE IN HONG KONG

Pictured above is the iconic view of the Twins hike, overlooking the Stanley neighborhood in the south side of Hong Kong island. With nearly 1,400 steps, the Twins is quite a steep hike but the views at the top makes the effort worth it.

My favorite hikes in Hong Kong:

Hike Name Location Difficulty
Twin Peaks Repulse Bay to Stanley 4/5
Dragons Back Tai Tam to Shek-O 2/5
Lugard Road The Peak 1/5
Mount Butler Tai Tam to Parkview 4/5
Lions Rock Sha Tin 3/5

Clear Water Bay Golf and Country Club (pictured above) is the golf club I play at in Hong Kong. Located on a the Clear Water Bay peninsula in Sai Kung, this golf club has a beautiful 18 hole course located right by the South China Sea.

My home, located in Tai Tam, overlooks Tai Tam Bay (pictured above) in the southern part of Hong Kong. This is not the typical view people picture when they think of Hong Kong.

The beach pictured above is of a beach in Stanley located below my friends house. During the pandemic, this was our hang out spot for the summer.

Tai Long Wan (pictured above) is a popular beach in Sai Kung, Hong Kong. Many Hong Kongers rent junk boats or yachts and sail out here for the day to enjoy white sand beaches and crystal clear water.
