HOME KONG

A Photographic Journey of the City I Call Home

Welcome to my webpage about Hong Kong! Hong Kong is an incredibly diverse and vibrant city located on the eastern Pearl River Delta on the southern coast of China. This webpage will offer a basic introduction to the bustling streets and beautiful hiking trails of Hong Kong.

WHAT YOU WILL FIND ON THIS WEBPAGE

The 'city' page will introduce a few of my favorite neighborhoods on Hong Kong island including Central, Wan Chai, Causeway Bay, Stanley, and Repulse Bay. To learn more about each neighborhood, make your way to the 'city' page.

Hong Kong is home to an incredible array of nature walks and beautiful scenery. The lush mountains located next to the golden beaches makes Hong Kong a paradise for anyone who enjoys the outdoors. The 'nature' page will share some of my favorite hiking trails and beaches in Hong Kong.
